## 2. Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) Compliance
The developer explicitly recognizes that this application locally processes Protected and Sensitive Personal Information under Philippine Law, including:
* **Learner Identification:** Names, LRN, Sex, Birth Dates, Birth Order, and Handedness.
* **Demographics:** Barangay, Municipality/City, Province, and Region.
* **Family Profiles:** Parents'/Guardians' names, ages, occupations, and educational attainment data.
* **Socio-environmental Data:** Number of siblings and school enrollment status.
* **Timestamps:** Record creation and update dates (`created_at`/`updated_at`).

All database parameters remain isolated **LOCALLY AND EXCLUSIVELY** on your host machine via an offline SQLite database. Because this is a free offline demo tool, the developer **CANNOT** view, intercept, modify, or recover any datasets or metrics you input.
