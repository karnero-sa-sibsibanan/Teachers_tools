3. PRIVACY & COMPLIANCE (PHILIPPINE DATA PRIVACY ACT - RA 10173)

Sensitive Personal Information: This application processes data defined under Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). Because this tool manages early childhood records, the user explicitly acknowledges that the following specific student attributes are collected, stored, and managed locally:

  • Child Identification Profiles: The child's full legal name, Learner Reference Number (LRN), biological sex, exact date of birth, birth order, and dominant handedness (left/right).
  • Developmental Score Profiles: Raw scores, scaled scores, standard scores, interpretation categories, and developmental milestone milestones achieved across all official ECD domains.
  • Demographic & Locality Data: Target school enrollment status, along with regional routing fields including Barangay, Municipality or City, Province, and Region.
  • Family & Guardian Context: Parents' or legal guardians' full names, ages, employment/occupational data, and highest educational attainment level.
  • Socio-Environmental Metrics: Total number of siblings within the household unit.
  • System Audit Fields: Automated created_at and updated_at system timestamps recording the exact moment records are entered or altered.
